Our Story

It all started with Kiwiburn.

Our first Kiwiburn experience was 10 years ago. Seeing hundreds of people together who love life and the environment as much as we do was inspiring. We came back with a mission - To make our carbon footprint as tiny as possible. We started by making our own tiny home on wheels and roadtripping around the country. Wherever we went, people asked us about our tiny house. And so Absolute Tiny Houses was born.


Ash is the innovative mastermind, and builds with earthquake-proofing and council regulations in mind. Ash is the solar expert, making sure that our solar kits are always up to date with the latest technology, at the best possible price. We pride ourselves in building the ultimate luxurious and quality Tiny Home or Portable Cabin for you. Just by viewing any of our buildings you will very quickly see that we take great pride in all our work and our attention to detail is very close to unbeatable.


We believe in transparency and building with no hidden costs. We don't scrimp on the good stuff, and we'll never cut corners.
